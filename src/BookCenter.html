What is Book Center?

BookCenter is your center for all things Books. We invite you to delve into each sub-section of out site, which range from tracking your books, to seeing analytics on your reading habits, to getting help with your reading goals, and even taking notes on your favorite books. Explore the various tools and features we offer to enhance your reading experience!

Getting Started

1. Bookeep

Bookeep was the central idea for our webpage. A place where you can track your reading completion, sorted and organized, and note of your word counts. Also, if you want to challenge yourself and your friends, you can set up a reading challenge for Words Read or Books Read! Bookeep will do all the calculations for you.

2. BookHelp

BookHelp is a section of our website that provides helpful tools and resources to enhance your reading experience. It includes features like a word-per-minute calculator, customizable timer for reading time, comprehension tests, goal settings for your reading speed or books read, and personalized book recommendations based on your completed reading reported on Bookeep.

3. BookStats

BookStats is a section of our website that provides analytics and statistics on your reading habits. It shows you how many books you've read, how many words you've read, goals completed, and other useful metrics to help you track your progress all presented in neat visualizations in the form of charts.

4. BookNotes

BookNotes is a section of our website where you can take notes on your favorite books. It allows you to keep track of your thoughts, key quotes, and important details about each book you read in an organized manner.
